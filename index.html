About me

Birthplace and Hometown

I was born on the 24th of April of 1989 at 4:12pm in Chula Vista, CA at Scripps Memorial Hospital on H Street. I grew up in Mexicali, BC, Mexico and have resided there for most of my life, except for the 2 years my family moved to Chula Vista back in 2001, giving me the opportunity of attending Middle School in the US. We moved back to Mexicali in 2003, and it wasn't until 2015 that I went back to living in California on and off up until this day.

Upbringing

I grew up being upper middle class. Always attending private schools and living in the nice neighborhoods of town. There were hard times for the family economy every now and then, but my dad always strived to continue to give us the quality of life we were accustomed to. We certainly didn't live in luxury, but there was never anything lacking in our lives.

Family and Civil Status

My nuclear family is composed of both my parents, a younger brother, and myself. My brother has been married to Liz (my sister-in-law) for 3 years now, and just last year had his first kid, a beautiful son by the name of Santiago. I’m not married, have never been married, nor have any kids. I am though in a committed relationship and will hopefully get married in the next couple of months.

Education

I studied medicine at Universidad Autónoma de Baja California, where I graduated with a score of 84.17, placing 18th out of 56. I decided to attend this school since it is considered amongst the top 10 in the country, it’s a public school so tuition wouldn’t be an issue, and it is located in my hometown. That way there wouldn’t be any excuses for me not to do well.
